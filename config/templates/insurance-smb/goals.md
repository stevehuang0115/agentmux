# Insurance SMB Team OKRs

## Objective 1: Operational Excellence in Claims & Underwriting
- **KR1**: Reduce average claim cycle time (intake to settlement) by 25%.
- **KR2**: Achieve a 98% accuracy rate in policy analysis and risk profiling.
- **KR3**: Zero compliance violations found in quarterly internal audits.

## Objective 2: Drive Growth and Client Retention
- **KR1**: Increase year-over-year client retention rate to 92%.
- **KR2**: Generate $100k in new premium through cross-selling/up-selling campaigns.
- **KR3**: Achieve a 20% conversion rate on new marketing leads.

## Objective 3: Superior Client Experience
- **KR1**: Maintain a Client Satisfaction Score (CSAT) of 4.8/5.0.
- **KR2**: Send 100% of renewal reminders at least 60 days before expiration.
- **KR3**: Reduce time-to-onboard new clients by 50% through workflow automation.

## Objective 4: Technology & AI Efficiency
- **KR1**: Successfully automate 70% of routine claims intake and validation.
- **KR2**: AI agents correctly flag 100% of potential high-risk or non-compliant files.
- **KR3**: Achieve a 5x ROI on AI token spend compared to traditional administrative labor.
