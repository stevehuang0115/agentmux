# Insurance Compliance Checklist

## 1. Licensing & Credentials
- [ ] Agent/Broker licenses are current in all states of operation.
- [ ] Continuing Education (CE) requirements met for all licensed staff.
- [ ] E&O insurance for the agency is active and sufficient.

## 2. Client Communications
- [ ] All solicitations include required state disclosures.
- [ ] Quotes clearly state they are "estimates" and not "binders."
- [ ] Policy changes are confirmed in writing within 24 hours.
- [ ] Record of "declination of coverage" maintained for all refused recommendations.

## 3. Data Privacy & Security
- [ ] PII (Personally Identifiable Information) is encrypted at rest and in transit.
- [ ] Multi-factor authentication (MFA) enabled for all agency systems.
- [ ] Annual privacy notice sent to all clients.
- [ ] Compliance with HIPAA (if handling health-related data) or state-specific laws (e.g., CCPA).

## 4. Claims Handling
- [ ] Claims acknowledged within state-mandated timeframes (usually 10-15 days).
- [ ] Clear documentation of all investigations and settlement decisions.
- [ ] No "unfair claims practices" (e.g., misrepresenting facts, low-balling offers).

## 5. Marketing & Advertising
- [ ] No "bait and switch" advertising.
- [ ] All testimonials are from real clients and used with permission.
- [ ] Comparison ads are fair and not misleading.
