# Insurance SMB Team Template

This template provides a comprehensive AI team for independent insurance agencies and brokerages. It automates critical operational tasks like claims processing, risk analysis, and compliance monitoring, while also driving growth through proactive client engagement.

## 🏗️ Team Composition
- **Claims Processor (Chris)**: Manages the claims lifecycle from intake to settlement.
- **Policy Analyst (Pat)**: Analyzes policies, identifies gaps, and conducts risk profiles.
- **Client Comm Manager (Charlie)**: Handles onboarding, renewals, and cross-selling.
- **Compliance Officer (Casey)**: Ensures all operations meet regulatory standards.
- **Marketing Specialist (Morgan)**: Produces educational content and manages leads.

## 🔄 Included Workflows
1. **Claims Processing**: End-to-end flow from claim intake to settlement calculation.
2. **New Client Onboarding**: Verifies documents, analyzes risk, and reviews for compliance.
3. **Renewal & Cross-Sell**: Identifies upcoming renewals and personalizes outreach with cross-sell options.

## 📚 Bundled Knowledge
- **Insurance Industry Context**: Terminology, core processes, and common insurance lines.
- **Compliance Checklist**: A rigorous guide for maintaining regulatory compliance.

## 🚀 Quick Start
To instantiate this team, use the Crewly CLI:
```bash
npx crewly create team --template insurance-smb
```
