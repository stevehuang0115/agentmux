# Education SMB Team OKRs

## Objective 1: Deliver High-Quality, Scalable Educational Content
- **KR1**: Complete the curriculum design and content production for [Course Name] within 30 days.
- **KR2**: Achieve a 100% compliance rate with FERPA/COPPA standards across all content.
- **KR3**: Maintain a minimum quality score of 4.5/5 across all peer reviews.

## Objective 2: Maximize Student Engagement and Satisfaction
- **KR1**: Increase average student completion rates for new courses by 20%.
- **KR2**: Reduce response time to student queries to under 4 hours on average.
- **KR3**: Maintain a Net Promoter Score (NPS) of 75 or higher.

## Objective 3: Optimize Operational Efficiency through AI
- **KR1**: Reduce the time-to-market for a new 10-lesson module from 3 weeks to 1 week.
- **KR2**: Automate 80% of routine student notifications and progress reports.
- **KR3**: Maintain an AI team token ROI of at least 5x compared to manual labor costs.
