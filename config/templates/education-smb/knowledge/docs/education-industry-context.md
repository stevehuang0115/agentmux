# Education Industry Context

## Common SOPs
1. **Curriculum Review Cycle**: Curricula should be reviewed every 6 months to ensure relevance and alignment with current standards.
2. **Student Privacy Protection**: All student data must be handled according to FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act) and COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) guidelines.
3. **Inclusive Design**: Educational materials must be accessible to students with diverse learning needs, following ADA (Americans with Disabilities Act) standards.

## Key Terminology
- **LMS (Learning Management System)**: Software for the administration, documentation, tracking, and delivery of educational courses.
- **SIS (Student Information System)**: Management information system for education establishments to manage student data.
- **IEP (Individualized Education Program)**: A document that is developed for each public school child who needs special education.
- **Pedagogy**: The method and practice of teaching, especially as an academic subject or theoretical concept.
- **Blended Learning**: A style of education in which students learn via electronic and online media as well as traditional face-to-face teaching.
