# Education Content Quality Standards

## 1. Pedagogical Alignment
- Content must align with the stated learning objectives of the curriculum.
- Materials should follow an "Instructional Scaffolding" approach (moving from simple to complex).

## 2. Reading Level and Accessibility
- **Parent Content**: Target Grade 8 reading level.
- **Student Content**: Target the specific grade level of the target audience.
- **Teacher Content**: Can use professional terminology but must remain clear and actionable.

## 3. Engagement and Interactivity
- Use active voice and direct address.
- Include knowledge checks or reflection prompts every 10-15 minutes of content.
- Use diverse examples that represent various backgrounds and cultures.

## 4. Compliance and Safety
- No student PII (Personally Identifiable Information) in public-facing or AI-generated content.
- All links must be verified for safety and relevance.
- Content must be free of bias and stereotypes.

## 5. Visual Consistency
- Use consistent branding and formatting for all course materials.
- All images must have alt-text for accessibility.
