## 6. The Caveats: Honesty Over Hype

At Crewly, we don't believe in "AI Magic." We believe in **AI Management.** While the cost savings are massive, it is critical to understand the limitations of a 92%-cheaper team.

### AI Agents Can't Do Everything (Yet)
1.  **High-Level Strategy:** An AI can summarize your data, but it cannot decide the vision of your company. You still need a human to set the "North Star."
2.  **The "Hallucination" Tax:** Agents occasionally fall into logic loops. We’ve seen **Sam (Dev)** try to fix a bug by refactoring the same 5 lines of code three times in a row.
3.  **The "Translation" Tone:** **Luna (Content)** can sometimes produce text that feels too corporate or slightly "AI-ish." This is why our v3 refinement workflow is mandatory.
4.  **Complex Architecture:** AI is great at building components, but "big picture" system design (e.g., migrating a monolithic database to microservices) still requires human oversight to avoid technical debt.

**The Golden Rule:** Treat AI agents like highly motivated, incredibly fast, but occasionally literal-minded **interns.** They need a manager, a clear brief, and a quality gate.
